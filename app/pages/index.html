Where I am

What cemented my decision to go into web development was the realization that the learning is never done. There is always a new technology, a new technique, new systems to explore and the nerd in me loves that. My apprenticeship at Bloc was wonderful but by allowing myself to look beyond the allocated work I’ve also taught myself the basics of data structures and algorithms, web accessible design, and I really drooled over Sass and CSS Grid. My to-do list of things to learn is always getting longer and I like that challenge.
(Plus, I make A-ma-zing chocolate chip cookies.)

Where I'm going

I'm entering the specialization portion of my Bloc.io education. In addition to a capstone project where I'll be making an ecommerce enabled website for a local Chicago business, I'll also be creating two additional front-end focused projects. Keep your eyes on my portfolio to read about my progress and see my work in progress. I'm also looking forward to completion of my program in August 2017!
